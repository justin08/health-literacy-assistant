# Water Safety (Recreational)

Playing in the water - whether swimming, boating or diving - can be fun. It can also be dangerous, especially for children. Being safe can help prevent injuries and drowning . To stay safe in the water: - Avoid alcohol when swimming or boating - Wear a life jacket whenever you're in a boat - Don't swim alone, or in bad weather - Learn CPR - Learn to swim and teach your children to swim - Supervise your children when they are in the water - Prevent sunburns - use plenty of sunscreen
