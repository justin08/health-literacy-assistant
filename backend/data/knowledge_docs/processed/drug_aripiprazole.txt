# Aripiprazole
Type: Medication / Drug Information

Aripiprazole is used to treat:   schizophrenia (a mental illness that affects how a person thinks, feels and behaves)  bipolar disorder (a disease that causes depression,  mania, and other abnormal moods)  major depressive disorder  irritability symptoms (aggression, temper tantrums, mood changes) in those with autistic disorder (a developmental problem that affects how individuals communicate, learn and behave)  Tourette's disorder (a condition causing involuntary, repetitive movements or sounds) Aripiprazole is in a class of medications called atypical antipsychotics. It works by changing the activity of certain natural substances in the brain.
