# Penis Disorders
Also known as: Penile disorders

Problems with the penis can cause pain and affect a man's sexual function and fertility. 
Penis disorders include: - Erectile dysfunction - inability to get or keep an 
erection - Priapism - a painful erection that does not go away - Peyronie's disease - bending of the penis during an erection due to a hard lump 
called a plaque - Balanitis - inflammation of the skin covering the head of the penis, most often in 
men and boys who have not been circumcised - Penile cancer - a rare form of cancer, highly curable when caught early

Source: https://medlineplus.gov/penisdisorders.html
